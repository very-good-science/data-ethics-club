# Mailing list

Sign up to the Data Ethics Club Mailing List to get a week-before and on-the-day email reminder with the upcoming meeting information. We also send out updates about events organised or recommended by others in the Data Ethics Club community. 

[Click here to go to JISCMail to sign up.](https://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/cgi-bin/webadmin?SUBED1=DATAETHICSCLUB&A=1)

The organisers listed on the [contact](./contact) page are the data controllers for this mailing list, and JISC Mail is the provider used to manage your subscription to the list. You may unsubscribe at any time, and we only use this list for relevant information about Data Ethics Club.
